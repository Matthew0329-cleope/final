The Ozobot Story

2012

Foundation

Nader Hamda founded Evollve Inc. with the mission to inspire young minds to go from consuming technology to creating it. The project was named Ozobot by his 10-year-old daughter.

Jan 2014

First Robot

Bit, the original Ozobot, was unveiled at CES in Las Vegas.

Aug 2014

Color Codes

Ozobot patented Color Codes, a screen-free way to code robots with markers and stickers.

2016

Evo Introduced

Evo, an app-connected Ozobot enhanced with Bluetooth Smart, a built-in speaker, and sensors, was unveiled.

2017-Present

Continued Innovation

Ozobot continues to release new educational content, software updates, and engage with the educational community to further STEAM learning.

About Ozobot

Ozobot, by Evollve Inc., is dedicated to creating innovative ways for technically minded youngsters to play, learn, and communicate in an ever-expanding digital world. Ozobot's mission is to inspire young minds to go from consuming technology to creating it. Their robots are designed to make learning about coding and robotics fun and accessible, particularly for children. Ozobot aims to redefine the role of robotics in education with award-winning programmable robots and STEAM-based learning solutions.

Nader Hamda founded Evollve Inc. in 2012. His vision was to create a company that blends creative thinking with business disciplines to bring new ideas to market, focusing initially on robotics for education. Under his leadership, Ozobot has become a leading name in educational robotics, empowering educators and engaging students with innovative learning tools.

Driving the Future of STEAM Education

Ozobot continues to be a leader in the STEAM education space, focusing on creating robots and learning resources that empower educators and inspire students.
